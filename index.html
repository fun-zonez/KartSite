About Brahmastra

Brahmastra is a team of passionate mechanical engineers dedicated to designing, fabricating, and testing high-performance go-karts. Our mission is to blend theory with hands-on learning, pushing the boundaries of mechanical design and motorsport engineering.

Our Vision

To create an environment where students learn by building ‚Äî transforming classroom knowledge into real-world engineering applications.

Our Mission

Promote hands-on mechanical learning, design and build competitive go-karts, represent our college at national-level competitions, and encourage teamwork, creativity, and safety in motorsport.

Focus Areas

Design & Fabrication

Advanced CAD modeling and precision manufacturing.

Mechanical Innovation

Research and development of cutting-edge technologies.

Racing Competitions

Participation in national-level go-kart championships.

Skill Development

Workshops, training, and collaborative learning.

Our Team

Meet the passionate engineers behind Brahmastra

Design Team

Chassis & Frame Design

Responsible for overall go-kart architecture using SolidWorks. Focus on weight optimization, strength, and aerodynamics.

Brake System Team

Safety & Control Systems

Designs complete brake system including calipers, discs, and hydraulic mechanisms.

Power Transmission Team

Drive & Power Transfer

Handles clutch, gearbox, driveshaft, and differential for efficient power delivery.

Steering Team

Control & Maneuverability

Designs steering mechanism for precise handling and responsive control.

Each team works collaboratively to ensure seamless integration of all systems. Currently, we have 20+ active members driving this passion forward.
