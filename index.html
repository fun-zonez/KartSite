About Brahmastra

Brahmastra is a team of passionate mechanical engineers dedicated to designing, fabricating, and testing high-performance go-karts. Our mission is to blend theory with hands-on learning, pushing the boundaries of mechanical design and motorsport engineering.

Our Vision

To create an environment where students learn by building — transforming classroom knowledge into real-world engineering applications.

Our Mission

Promote hands-on mechanical learning, design and build competitive go-karts, represent our college at national-level competitions, and encourage teamwork, creativity, and safety in motorsport.

Focus Areas

Design & Fabrication

Advanced CAD modeling and precision manufacturing.

Mechanical Innovation

Research and development of cutting-edge technologies.

Racing Competitions

Participation in national-level go-kart championships.

Skill Development

Workshops, training, and collaborative learning.

Our Projects

Brahmastra Kart

High-performance racing kart designed using SolidWorks and analyzed with ANSYS. Currently in prototype phase with 20+ member team collaboration.

Frame Design

Advanced frame design using SolidWorks with comprehensive ANSYS analysis for structural integrity and safety.

Completed Prototype

Our first prototype has been completed with rigorous testing and validation using our advanced simulation tools.

Our Technology Stack

⚙️ SolidWorks

Used for frame design and 3D modeling of go-kart components

🔍 ANSYS

For structural analysis and simulation of designs

📐 CAD/CAM

Computer-aided design and manufacturing tools

📊 Simulation

Advanced simulation and testing of prototypes
